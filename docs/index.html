BUNNOS

Rabbits have seven letters, so does healthy.

Bunnos is an online marketplace specifically for sales

of breeding rabbits and meat rabbits.

This project is aimed at enthusiasts willing to

purchase rabbits at ease for breeding and meat

purposes respectively.

Bunnos as an online marketplace has introduced some key features for easy posting of ads and buying into such ads.

This section highlights three(3) key features implemented on Bunnos

  • Website flexibility in navigation for guests
  • User Authentication
  • Escrow Service between Buyer and seller

Website flexibility in navigation

Bunnos as an online marketplace provides the flexibility for both buyers and sellers on the website to navigate some of the areas without much restriction. Buyers wanting to contact a seller of an ad need not sign-up to view ads related to their search results from the homepage. They simply need to search a keyword related to their need and view through various ads returned back to them.

Although for cautionary scenarios of scam sellers, such user must sign-up to enjoy Bunnos escrow service that guarantees an easy and smooth transaction between both parties. This is the most recommended action implored to buyer of an ad to take

User Authentication

Bunnos marketplace has implemented a mandatory sign-up process for users desiring to post ads on the website. This has been done to be able to store each users ad easily for retrieving and matching for prospective buyers to easy contact with them.

This also helps registered users to monitor activities like feedback and Client list with succesful transactions

Escrow Service between buyer and seller

Bunnos Marketplace utilizes the use of escrow for both registered buyer and seller. This process ensures that both the seller and buyer finish their transaction and are both satisfactory. The detailed process on the website's homepage explains what is to be done by both parties before a transaction is marked completed.

What inspired Bunnos?

While Bunnos is a portfolio project for ALX-Africa Group in conjunction with Holberton School of Tulsa. Being a rabbit enthusiast from Oyo State, Nigeria, I have grown fond of rabbits as pets and meat consumption. People think rabbit to be an expensive animal to consume or cater for while neglicting the various advantages they provide being an animal with white meat.

As a Rabbit farmer for more than 5 years experience, Rabbits are generally one of the easiest animal to breed, ranging from feed consumption, mating, wastes(which can be used for fertizer and manure).

This website is designed to make buying and selling of Rabbits easy in my locale and to serve as awareness to the populace such an advantageous species are not expensive to venture into
